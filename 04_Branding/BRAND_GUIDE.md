# Immersive EGO: CIVITAS — Brand guide

## Identity

- **Tagline:** *Colony • Family • Work • Faction • War*
- **Role:** Societal expansion of Immersive EGO.

## Symbolism

- Three towers represent MineColonies, MCA Reborn and Modern Companions.
- The central EGO eye represents continuity with body and mind simulation.
- Three linked civic nodes represent household, faction and colony.
- The shield represents governance, protection and collective identity.
