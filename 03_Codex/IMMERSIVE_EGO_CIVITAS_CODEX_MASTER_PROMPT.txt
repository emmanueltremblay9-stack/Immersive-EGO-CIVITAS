======================================================================
1. PRODUCT MISSION
======================================================================

CIVITAS is a mandatory integration of four first-class pillars:

1. MineColonies
   Colony, territory, buildings, housing, jobs, work orders, requests, warehouse,
   couriers, permissions, raids, and guards.

2. MCA Reborn
   Human NPC identity, appearance, personality, mood, relationships, marriage,
   family, age, life stage, and dialogue.

3. Modern Companions
   Combat roles, follow/guard/patrol/escort/hold/retreat orders, equipment,
   experience, traits, morale, bond, friendly-fire rules, and safe recall,
   adapted into host-neutral services.

4. Immersive EGO
   Physiology, hydration, nutrition, sleep, fatigue, stress, biome origin,
   acclimation, and readiness.

CIVITAS owns:
- canonical civic resident identity;
- host adapters and cross-system orchestration;
- professions, specializations, skills, and schedules;
- household mapping;
- culture, political faction, loyalty, grievances, and reputation;
- transaction ledger and generalized work recipes;
- economy, barter, favor, debt, and contracts;
- governance, laws, crime, justice, migration, crisis, and diplomacy.

The result must be one coherent society, not four duplicate simulations.
