# Asset Policy

No upstream assets are approved for copying into CIVITAS by default.

Forbidden by default:

- logos and trademarks;
- textures;
- sounds;
- skins;
- models;
- structures and NBT templates;
- GUI art;
- screenshots;
- generated art from upstream branding.

CIVITAS may use original assets under the `immersive_ego_civitas` namespace.
Installed runtime dependencies provide their own assets externally.

The provenance gate fails if resources are committed under upstream namespaces
such as `minecolonies`, `mca`, `modern_companions`, `humancompanions`, or
`basicweapons`.
