# Product scope

## Included

- Unified resident records across MineColonies, MCA and Modern Companions hosts.
- MineColonies-backed colony membership, housing, work, storage, permissions and raids.
- MCA-backed identity, relationships, family, life stage and dialogue.
- Modern Companions-derived roles, orders, equipment, XP, traits, morale and bond.
- Immersive EGO needs/readiness for residents.
- Professions, specializations, skills, schedules and apprenticeship.
- NPC work recipes using existing workstations.
- Biome cultures, political factions, reputation, loyalty and grievances.
- Scarcity, barter, favor, debt, contracts and dynamic value.
- Households, education, migration, governance, law, crime, crises and diplomacy.
- Original dashboards and exhaustive configuration.

## Excluded from core scope

- New CIVITAS blocks, items, fluids or entity families.
- New Blockbench models.
- Copies of upstream art, logos or branding.
- Destructive MCA-to-MineColonies entity conversion.
- A second independent family graph.
- A second independent colony authority.
- Shading or JarJar of mandatory mods.
- Cold Sweat.
- Medical, hygiene, bladder or amusement simulation.
- Cross-loader support.

## Deferred compatibility

- Optional currency providers such as Lightman's Currency.
- Optional dialogue providers such as Easy NPC.
- Other colony or worker mods.
