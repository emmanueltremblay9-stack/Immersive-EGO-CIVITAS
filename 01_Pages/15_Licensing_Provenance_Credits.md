# Licensing, provenance and credits

## Project license

`GPL-3.0-only`

## Adapted-source record

Every copied or substantially adapted file must record:

- upstream project;
- immutable commit;
- original path;
- original copyright/authors;
- license;
- destination;
- changes;
- reviewer;
- asset involvement.

## Asset policy

Installed dependencies provide their own assets. CIVITAS uses original branding and original UI resources. No upstream logo, model, texture, sound, skin, structure or GUI art is copied by default.
