# MCA Reborn integration

MCA is mandatory and remains authoritative for social life.

## Preserve

- Human appearance and identity.
- Name.
- Personality and mood.
- Player relationship.
- Marriage and kinship.
- Parent/child/sibling relationships.
- Age and life stage.
- Dialogue and social history.

## CIVITAS adds

- Colony membership.
- Household and shared home.
- Profession and skills.
- Culture and political faction.
- Loyalty, grievances and civic reputation.
- Work and military assignments.
- Economic and governance state.

## Hard rules

- Never store a second spouse field.
- Never construct a parallel family graph.
- Never reset relationship progress during recruitment.
- Prefer MCA dialogue hooks for recruitment, contracts and civic choices.
- Children receive explicit, configurable safety and life-stage gates.
