# Factions, culture and reputation

## Distinct concepts

| Concept | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Origin | Biological/environmental adaptation from Immersive EGO |
| Culture | Learned communal habits |
| Faction | Political affiliation |
| Colony | Concrete MineColonies settlement |
| Profession | Economic role |
| Family | MCA kinship network |

## Reputation dimensions

- Trust
- Respect
- Fear
- Honor
- Crime
- Favor
- Debt
- Legitimacy
- Loyalty

Fear must not silently substitute for trust. High fear with low trust creates unstable obedience and greater revolt/desertion pressure.

## Culture formation

Culture can seed from foundation biome, population origins, resources and dominant professions. It then evolves slowly through migration, trade, success and crisis.
