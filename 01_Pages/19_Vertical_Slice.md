# Desert colony vertical slice

## Scenario

A MineColonies desert settlement contains housing, warehouse, farm and guard infrastructure. An MCA villager with a family is recruited.

## Required flow

1. Create one canonical resident record.
2. Preserve MCA host and social identity.
3. Add MineColonies colony membership non-destructively.
4. Assign family-aware housing.
5. Assign a compatible profession and building.
6. Read Immersive EGO hydration, fatigue, sleep and readiness.
7. Create and fulfill a warehouse request.
8. Complete one work cycle.
9. Apply drought scarcity.
10. Change schedule, output and market value.
11. Assign a guard/patrol role.
12. Survive a raid while civilians shelter.
13. Update trust, respect, favor, legitimacy and migration pressure.
14. Save, restart and verify every authority.

## Exit rule

The slice is complete only on a dedicated server with no item duplication, family duplication, identity loss or stale transaction.
