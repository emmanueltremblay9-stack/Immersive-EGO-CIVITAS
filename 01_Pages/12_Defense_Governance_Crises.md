# Defense, governance and crises

## Defense

- Guards and militia.
- Civilian shelter.
- Protect-family priorities.
- Patrols and escorts.
- Commander roles.
- Raid response.
- Fatigue, casualty and trauma consequences.

## Governance

- Leaders, councils and guild influence.
- Legitimacy.
- Configurable laws.
- Crime memory and justice responses.
- Succession.
- Migration, exile and asylum.

## Crisis model

A crisis has six stages:

```text
trigger → warning → escalation → intervention → recovery → historical memory
```

Initial profiles:

- drought;
- famine;
- raid trauma;
- guard exhaustion;
- labor shortage/strike;
- trade collapse;
- succession;
- migration wave.
