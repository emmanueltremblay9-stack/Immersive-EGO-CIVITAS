# Work, logistics and economy

## Worker equation

```text
profession + skill + building + workstation + inputs + tools
+ EGO readiness + schedule + safety + culture + reputation
= duration + quality + output + experience
```

## NPC work recipes

Data-driven recipes may define:

- profession and specialization;
- MineColonies building requirement;
- existing workstation tags;
- input reservations;
- tools;
- stages and duration;
- skill/readiness/safety thresholds;
- culture, faction or reputation requirements;
- output and quality.

## Logistics lifecycle

```text
locate → reserve → assign → move → process → consume → output → release
```

Death, unload, job change, cancellation and restart must be idempotent and repairable.

## Economy

Value may derive from:

- warehouse stock;
- population need;
- biome and season;
- crisis;
- route risk and distance;
- culture;
- reputation;
- favor and debt.

Emeralds remain valid, but they are not the only exchange mechanism.
