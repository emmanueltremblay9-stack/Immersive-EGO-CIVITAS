# Executive vision

CIVITAS is neither another NPC mod nor a thin compatibility bridge. It is a GPL integration project that turns separate simulation systems into a persistent society.

Every resident receives:

- a MineColonies civic context;
- an MCA social identity and family;
- a Modern Companions-derived military capability;
- an Immersive EGO physiological and psychological state;
- a CIVITAS household, profession, culture, faction, reputation, economy and political history.

## Player promise

The player does not merely place buildings or collect followers. The player governs a society whose physical survival produces economic, social, military and political consequences.

A drought can:

```text
reduce hydration
→ lower readiness
→ slow production
→ reduce warehouse stock
→ raise prices
→ create debt and grievances
→ weaken legitimacy
→ increase migration pressure
→ make a raid harder to survive
```

## Strategic distinction

| System | Primary strength |
|---|---|
| MineColonies | Colony construction, workers and logistics |
| MCA Reborn | Human identity, family and relationships |
| Modern Companions | Combat roles, orders and progression |
| Immersive EGO | Individual survival and psychology |
| CIVITAS | One civic organism using all four |
